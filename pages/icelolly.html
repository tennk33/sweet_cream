ICELOLLY

üç° SWEET & ICY

An ice lolly, ice lollipop, or ice pop, is a frozen snack of flavoured ice. It is made by freezing flavoured liquid (such as fruit juice) or cream around a wooden stick. Once the liquid freezes solid, the stick can be used as a handle to hold the ice pop.

Lollies take 4-8 hours to freeze depending on the ingredients you use. The higher the water-to-sugar ratio the faster your lolly will freeze. Alcohol will slow the process and too much will result in a slushy lolly.

What is the healthiest ice lolly? The healthiest and best ice lolly or ice cream overall is the Mr Freeze Jubbly Strawberry, without a doubt.
