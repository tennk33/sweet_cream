Nutrition

1. Does Sweet Cream offer no-sugar-added ice cream flavors?

We offer two no sugar added flavors, please note these have naturally occurring sugars. Please check back as we often add new product flavors.

2. Do Sweet Cream products contain whey?

All of our ice cream is made using real milk. Since whey is a derivative of real milk our ice creams contain whey. For whey-free options, try our dairy-free sorbets and ices.

Coupons

1. Does Sweet Cream offer coupons?

Yes! Check our Event page for select promotions. Also, download the Mobile app and sign up for our emails to receive additional offers.

Birthday Club

1. What is theBirthday Club?

The Birthday Club is a free program for our biggest fans. Join and receive special birthday offers. You may also opt-in to our email program, or download our mobile app to receive offers and promotions throughout the year.

2. Is the Birthday Club just for kids?

No way! The Birthday Club is for anyone who loves ice cream; however, you must be 13 or older to join.

3. How do I sign up for the Birthday Club?

Joining the Birthday Club is free and easy! First, create a account. Then complete your profile to join the Birthday Club. (You must be 13 or older to sign up.)

4. How will I get my birthday coupons?

Your birthday coupons will be sent via email.

Guest Survey Redemption

1. How do I redeem my guest survey coupon?

Simply bring your receipt with the validation code filled in to the shop listed at the top of the receipt.

Purchasing Gift Cards

1. Does Sweet Cream sell gift cards?

Yes. You can purchase standard gift cards in our shops and on the Mobile app, and custom gift cards online through our trusted third-party vendor, in any denomination between $2 and $100.

2. Where can I buy a Baskin-Robbins gift card?

You can purchase gift cards at a our shop near you. You can also purchase mobile gift cards in the Mobile app.

Redeeming Gift Cards

1. What if my gift card is lost or stolen?

Please treat your gift card like cash. We cannot replace it if it is lost or stolen. However, you can register your gift card in the Mobile app to keep track of it digitally.

2. Do Sweet Cream gift cards expire?

No. Sweet Cream gift cards do not expire.
